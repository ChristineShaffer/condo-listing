FAQ

What are the condo fees?

The condo fees run $431 per month for this unit and include heat, water, and garbage.

How many parking stalls does this unit have?

One, oversized, titled stall. The building also has guest parking in the underground, and street parking out front. There are additional stalls available to rent in the building.

What are the pet restrictions for this building?

Pets in the building are restricted to either one cat, or one dog not to exceeding 15 inches at the shoulder and 30 lbs, aquarium animals, or birds.
